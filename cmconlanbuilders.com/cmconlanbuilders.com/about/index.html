About

Meet Chris Conlan, the head of C. M. Conlan Contractors and Builders, a company he founded in 1989. With over 30 years of experience as a residential home builder and a background in real estate, Chris has developed a reputation for his expert craftsmanship, close attention to detail, and exceptional strength in design. He is an award-winning builder recognized by the Montgomery County Builder’s Association and has been featured in Bethesda Magazine as one of the area’s top builders.

C. M. Conlan Contractors and Builders is a Bethesda-based firm that also builds in Chevy Chase, Potomac, Rockville, and the larger Montgomery County area. We work with families to build custom homes tailored to your specific needs either on a lot we help you find, a lot you currently own, or one of the lots we have available for purchase. You can also choose from any of our quality existing homes currently for sale at competitive prices, available for viewing on Our Homes page. If you are a homeowner who is looking to upgrade, we offer expert renovations and additions as well. Feel free to give us a call — we are happy to meet for estimates and initial consultations.

Here at C. M. Conlan Contractors and Builders, we pride ourselves on offering our customers a superior building experience. We believe in developing a strong relationship between the builder and the client. You can depend on Chris to work with you closely throughout the process, and to be active and accessible from start to finish. We take a hands-on role in the design process, and Chris will meet with you to help you design the perfect home for you, or to help you choose from one of the many popular designs we have on hand. We have a strong reputation for quickly and efficiently building homes of superior quality, so we can settle your family into the home of your dreams in no time. Our homes are serviced with an extensive full-year warranty, but you can count on Chris to be consistently available for any questions or needs you may have as a homeowner.

Our company is also a proud supporter of Rebuilding Together Montgomery County, a local nonprofit that specializes in providing renovations and housing support for underprivileged families in our community. Chris has been an active board member of RTMC, the leader of multiple home renovation projects in the area, and a proud participant in the RTMC Playhouse Project. We are proud to give the gift of a home to those in need.

For more information, contact us using the button below. We look forward to building with you!
